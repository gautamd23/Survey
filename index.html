March 02
The day it all started.

If you’re reading this, maybe you were just curious or maybe something reminded you of me. I don’t know if we’ll ever talk the way we used to, but I want you to know I’ve been reflecting a lot. I see my mistakes more clearly now, and I’m truly sorry for the ways I hurt you, intentionally or unintentionally. I miss you not just the relationship, but you as a person. I’m working on becoming better, not to win you back, but because I understand now what I should have done differently. You don’t owe me a response. I just didn’t want my silence to ever mean I didn’t care. If someday you ever feel like talking again, I promise I’ll listen differently. And if that day never comes, I’ll still always be grateful for what we had.

— Gautam
